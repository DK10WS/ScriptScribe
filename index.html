About

Who we are

At Scribescript, we empower clear communication and knowledge access. Our frustration with managing handwritten notes sparked the creation of this user-friendly platform. Whether you're a student managing notes, a busy professional, or someone who simply wants to digitize handwritten content, Scribescript is here to help.

What we do

Effortless Summarization: Scribescript's summarization tool saves you time by automatically generating concise and informative summaries. Just input your text and let our AI do the heavy lifting!
‍Handwriting Recognition: Upload PDFs or images containing handwritten text, and our powerful recognition engine will convert them to editable digital text. This allows you to seamlessly edit, share, or summarize your handwritten content.
